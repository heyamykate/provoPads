Once upon a time, in an apartment far, far away...

One of the biggest pain points in Provo is apartment hunting.

When I was a student in Provo, I spent hours going through Craigslist and KSL, trying to sift through the apartment listings to find a suitable place to rest my weary head. I knew there had to be an easier way to do it. If I could order Cafe Rio online, there had to be a way for me to easily track down an apartment, without being asked to wire money to Nigeria.

Enter Provo Pads.

I can't solve all the world's problems, but I hope that through Provo Pads, I can help BYU students spend less time searching for an apartment, and more time swiping through Tinder studying.

Happy Hunting,
Amy-Kate
